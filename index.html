AP Marketing & Sales: 4CID Prototypes

Two interactive prototypes exploring how the 4C/ID model could structure performance tasks in a proposed AP Marketing & Sales course.

  • Sales Prototype — Live discovery call simulation with scaffolded tiers of complexity.
  • Marketing Prototype — Campaign brief portfolio builder with tiered learning tasks.

Low-fidelity prototypes meant to demonstrate the shape of a 4CID-aligned learner experience. Not a production product.
